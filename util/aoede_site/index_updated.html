AOEDE (ay-EE-dee) - Language Study Reinvented

AOEDE was the name of an ancient Greek muse, a source of creative inspiration.

Now experience your own inspiration with the AOEDE app, a revolutionary tool for studying any language through reading, and listening, practice.

Key Features:

  • The AOEDE app automatically displays in your own language.
  • To get started with AOEDE, select the language you want to study, your desired reading level (from beginner to advanced), and any of dozens of classic literary choices. Then click Load Book to see the first sentence, simplified according to your selected reading level. Click Next Sentence to proceed onto the next sentence.
  • AOEDE allows you to view each sentence in your study language, or your own language, or both. You can even practice listening for comprehension without seeing the written sentences at all.
  • AOEDE's Listen button plays each foreign sentence with the simulated voice of a native speaker, adjustable from slow to fast.
  • AOEDE remembers where you are in each book, even across sessions. Read as many books as you like, at the same time, or one after another. These are complete books. You'll never run out of interesting material to practice with.
