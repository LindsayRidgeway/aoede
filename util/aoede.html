AOEDE

Key Features:

The AOEDE app automatically adapts to your native language, ensuring ease of use for learners around the globe. Simply choose your preferred study language, reading level, and source material. As you progress, the app remembers your place, enabling seamless transitions between sessions.

  • Automatic language detection for user interface
  • Easy toggling of sentence visibility and translations
  • Adjustable reading speeds to suit individual learning paces
  • Convenient "Rewind" feature to revisit earlier parts of the text
  • Persistent memory of your progress across multiple books and sessions
