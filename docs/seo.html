Aoede

[ay-EE-dee]

Aoede is an immersive language practice app that helps you read and listen to real books one sentence at a time.

Each sentence is spoken aloud in a native voice. You can see the sentence and its translation — or hide either one. You can listen as many times as you want.

The book does not have to be in the language you're studying — Aoede translates it for you.

You control:

  • Your study language
  • Your reading level
  • Your listening speed
  • The book you want to read
