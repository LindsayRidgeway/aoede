AOEDE (ay-EE-dee) - A Language Study App

Named for the ancient Greek muse of voice and song — reimagined for the language learners of today

Key Features:

  • Study any language by reading and listening to classic works of literature.
  • Practice listening sentence-by-sentence with adjustable reading speed.
  • View or hide text in your study language, your own language, or both ...
  • ... or neither! So you can practice just listening to the book translated into your study language.
  • Hear the voice of a native speaker of your study language.
  • Persistent session memory allows you to pick up where you left off in each book.
  • Automatically detects and adapts the app to your own language (disabled in beta versions).
  • Optional sentence auto-play.
  • Optional individual word articulation.
  • Add and delete books from your library.
  • Forward and backward navigation thru book.
  • Supports input control with a gamepad controller.
  • Fully responsive for web and mobile use.
  • Clean, intuitive UI for focus and flow.
