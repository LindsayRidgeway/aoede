Key Features:

  • Study any language by reading classic works of literature.
  • Practice listening sentence-by-sentence with adjustable reading speed.
  • View or hide text in your study language, your own language, or both ...
  • ... or neither! So you can practice just listening to the book translated into your study language.
  • Uses the voice of a native speaker of your study language.
  • Persistent session memory allows you to pick up where you left off in each book.
  • Automatically detects and adapts the app to your own language.
  • Fully responsive for web and mobile use.
  • Clean, intuitive UI for focus and flow.
Note: The AOEDE app automatically displays in your own language. This feature is disabled in the beta versions.
